Europa Conference League

The UEFA Europa Conference League is an annual football club competition organized by the Union of European Football Associations (UEFA). It is the third-tier competition of European club football, ranking below the UEFA Champions League and the UEFA Europa League.

History

The competition was introduced in 2021 as part of UEFA's restructuring of its club competitions. The Europa Conference League aims to give more clubs from more countries the chance to participate in European competitions.

Format

The competition consists of several stages, including qualifying rounds, a group stage, knockout rounds, and a final. Teams qualify for the competition based on their performance in their national leagues and cup competitions. The group stage is followed by a knockout phase, culminating in the final held at a neutral venue.

Recent Champions

  • 2024:Olympiacos

  • 2023: West Ham United

  • 2022: AS Roma
